⚔️ JUNIORS TIER (Ages 8–12)
Game Type: RPG Adventure — quests, manual battles, kingdom building, overworld map
The Star of the Pitch ⭐
Core Game Loop:
Complete tasks → earn energy → explore overworld map → fight weak enemies → defeat 5 normal enemies per region → big boss unlocks → defeat boss → earn building materials → kingdom grows → new region unlocks → repeat
Energy System:
	•	Every map action costs energy
	•	Completing a real task refills energy
	•	Parent can grant bonus energy as a reward
	•	No automatic refill — tied directly to real tasks
The 5 Regions (unlock in order):
	1.	🌿 Grass — home region, bandits/wolves/goblins, Boss: The Bandit King
	2.	🌊 Water — crabs/jellyfish/pirates, Boss: The Kraken Lord
	3.	🏜️ Desert — scorpions/sand warriors/serpents, Boss: The Sand Pharaoh
	4.	❄️ Snow — ice wolves/frost giants/yetis, Boss: The Frost Queen
	5.	🌋 Volcano — fire demons/lava beasts/dragons, Boss: The Dragon King
Each Region Contains:
	•	3 weak enemy types roaming freely — unlimited, easy fights, cost small energy
	•	5 normal enemies to track and defeat — harder, scaled to player level
	•	1 big boss — appears only after all 5 normal enemies are defeated, gives biggest rewards
Manual Battle System (player chooses every action, nothing is automatic):
	•	⚔️ Attack — deal damage based on level and equipped weapon
	•	🛡️ Defend — reduce incoming damage this turn
	•	🧪 Use Item — use a potion or special item
5 Character Choices (parent picks at setup):
	•	⚔️ Knight — sword + armor upgrades
	•	🧙 Wizard — staff + spell upgrades
	•	👑 Royal — scepter + robe upgrades
	•	🌿 Ranger — bow + cloak upgrades
	•	🌊 Captain — cutlass + coat upgrades
Collectible Battle Items:
	•	🧪 Health Potion — restore health during battle
	•	⚡ Power Surge — double attack damage for one turn
	•	🛡️ Iron Shield — block all damage for one turn
	•	✨ Lucky Charm — guaranteed hit on next attack
Kingdom Building (permanent, never resets):
Structures unlock one at a time as bosses are defeated:
Wooden watchtower → Stone walls → Main gate → Barracks → Market stall → Castle keep → Castle towers → Castle courtyard → Royal garden → Fully fortified castle (Version 1 end goal)
