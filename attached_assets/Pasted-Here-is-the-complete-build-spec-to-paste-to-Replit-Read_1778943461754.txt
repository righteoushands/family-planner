PROJECT: Rule of Life Wizard Version 2 + Chores Expansion
PHILOSOPHY: This wizard is built on the framework from A Plan for Joy in the Home by Laura Dominick. Seven commitments anchor everything: Wake up on time. Be faithful to your prayer plan — morning, noon, and night. Stick to getting your basic chores done every day. Be flexible when God steps in and alters your plan. Look back to your schedule to regain control of your day if you lose it. Spend time with your husband. Go to bed on time. These appear on the landing screen with attribution and are checked against by the AI at the end.
