What and why: the current system regenerates school assignments daily from the live cursor causing instability. This build creates a weekly plan that Lauren approves on Monday morning. Once approved the plan is frozen for the week. Lauren sees a review card on her dashboard showing Monday’s full plan with subject names week/day badges and lesson text. She can approve or regenerate. Once approved the plan is frozen in data/school_week_plan.json and the boys’ PODs read from it. Before approval the boys see their assignments with a yellow “📋 Pending Mom’s approval — assignments may change” banner. After approval the banner disappears and assignments are stable for the week.
