1. Weekly Protein Structure (Locked Framework)

Rule: Every weekly menu must follow this protein rhythm:

1 red meat meal per week
3 poultry meals per week
2 fish meals per week
At least 2 legume-based meals per week

Details:

Fish is fixed on Fridays (Lenten compliance when applicable)
Legumes can overlap (e.g., black-eyed pea jambalaya counts toward both legume + protein)
Avoid clustering same proteins back-to-back unless intentional
🐟 2. Friday Rule (Non-Negotiable)

Rule: Fridays are meatless (especially during Lent)

Details:

Default: fish dinner (e.g., fried flounder)
Alternate: vegetarian meal (e.g., tomato soup + grilled cheese)
Lunch option: sardine “tuna-style” sandwiches
🍲 3. Soup System (Daily Anchor)

Rule: Keep a batch-cooked soup available daily

Purpose:

Backup meal
Easy lunch base
Side for dinner

Examples:

Chicken chili
Tomato soup
Beef stew
Lentil soup
🍗 4. Weekly Anchor Meals

Rule: Include these core meals each week:

1 brined roast chicken
1 roast beef meal
1 fish fry night (Friday)

Notes:

These act as “anchors” around which the rest of the menu is built
🍚 5. Carb Cycling (Dad’s Workout Alignment)

Rule: Align carbs with lifting days

Higher carb meals → Tuesday & Thursday (lifting days)
Lower/moderate carbs → other days

Examples:

High carb: rice, potatoes, bread
Lower carb: protein + vegetables
🍽 6. Whole Foods Priority

Rule: Prioritize minimally processed whole foods

Guidelines:

Favor real ingredients over packaged meals
Avoid ultra-processed foods when possible
Use evidence-based nutrition principles
🧺 7. Inventory-First Planning

Rule: Always plan meals starting with what you already have

Process:

Use fresh foods first (highest priority)
Use “about to expire” items next
Pull from freezer
Fill gaps with grocery list

Standing rule:

Prioritize anything explicitly marked as “use soon”
🛒 8. Grocery System Integration

Rule: Build grocery list directly from menu gaps

Details:

Only add what is missing
Tie items to specific meals
Surface Wegmans as a primary store
Use app-friendly structure when possible

Rule: Once the menu is set, do not change meals casually

Details:

No substitutions unless explicitly decided

Rule: Every meal must include prep steps earlier in the day
