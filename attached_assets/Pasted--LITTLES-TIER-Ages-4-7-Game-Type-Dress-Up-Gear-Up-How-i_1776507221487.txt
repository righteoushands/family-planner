🌟 LITTLES TIER (Ages 4–7)
Game Type: Dress-Up & Gear-Up
How it works: Parent marks tasks done (or enables child self-completion). Points fill a progress bar. When bar fills, 1 item unlocks, celebration animation plays, bar resets. Endless levels.
4 Game Choices (parent picks one per child at setup):
👑 Princess
Character + castle both grow more beautiful each level.
Level 1: Simple dress | Level 2: Pretty shoes | Level 3: Small crown | Level 4: New hairstyle | Level 5: Castle gets a garden | Level 6: Nicer dress | Level 7: A friend appears | Level 8: Castle gets a tower | Level 9: Sparkling accessories | Level 10: Ball gown (big upgrade) | Level 11+: Pattern repeats, always something new
🧜 Mermaid
Character + coral palace both grow more magical each level.
Level 1: Simple tail | Level 2: Pearl necklace | Level 3: Sea flower crown | Level 4: New hairstyle | Level 5: Coral palace gets a new room | Level 6: Sparkling tail upgrade | Level 7: A sea creature friend appears | Level 8: Palace gets a pearl gate | Level 9: Glowing accessories | Level 10: Beautiful gown top (big upgrade) | Level 11+: Pattern repeats, always something new
⚔️ Knight
Character armor + kingdom both grow stronger each level.
Level 1: Basic armor | Level 2: Sword upgrade | Level 3: Shield added | Level 4: Helmet upgrade | Level 5: Kingdom gets a new tower | Level 6: Armor gets polished and shinier | Level 7: A loyal horse appears | Level 8: Kingdom gets castle walls | Level 9: Cape added to armor | Level 10: Full shining knight upgrade (big upgrade) | Level 11+: Pattern repeats, always something new
🧙 Wizard
Character robes + enchanted world both grow more magical each level.
Level 1: Simple robe | Level 2: Magic staff | Level 3: Pointed hat | Level 4: New robe color | Level 5: Enchanted world gets a magic tree | Level 6: Staff gets a glowing crystal | Level 7: A magical creature appears (owl or dragon) | Level 8: Enchanted world gets a potion tower | Level 9: Robe gets stars and moons | Level 10: Full sorcerer upgrade (big upgrade) | Level 11+: Pattern repeats, always something new
